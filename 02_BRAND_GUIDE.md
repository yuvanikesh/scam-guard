1. Brand Overview

Product Name

AI-Dicto

⸻

Tagline

Get the Right One.

⸻

Extended Tagline

Describe Your Goal.
Get the Right Tool.

⸻

Brand Story

Where AI tools pile up, but ideas shouldn’t.

AI-Dicto exists to eliminate decision fatigue in the rapidly growing AI ecosystem.

Rather than overwhelming users with hundreds of tools, AI-Dicto guides them through conversation and recommendation to discover the most suitable AI solution for their goal.

⸻

2. Brand Mission

Help users make faster and better decisions when choosing AI tools.

⸻

3. Brand Vision

Become the most intuitive AI tool discovery platform by replacing browsing with conversation.

6. Positioning Statement

For users overwhelmed by AI tool choices,

AI-Dicto is a conversational AI tool discovery platform that recommends the most relevant tools based on goals and context,

unlike traditional AI directories that only provide search and filtering.

* Stock photos
* Corporate office photos
* Fake AI robots

* Depth layers

* Typography-driven layouts

18. Illustration Style

* Geometric
* Flat
* Clean

Avoid cartoon styles.

24. Final Brand Statement

AI-Dicto is a premium conversational AI tool discovery platform designed to help users find the right AI solution without navigating overwhelming directories. Through intelligent recommendations, voice interaction, and minimal design, AI-Dicto transforms AI tool discovery into a guided decision-making experience.
